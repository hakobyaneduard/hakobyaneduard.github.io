About us

KaTun Hostel is your home in Armenia. The main goal is to make our guests feel like at home. Everything is made for your comfort and good stay. Sleeping areas are very cosy and sunny, made with bright colors, so that you feel the summer. Beds are wide and soft, you will have your personal large locker in the room. The hostel has a large kitchen, where every morning from 9 am to 11 am a big, tasty and healthy breakfast is served. You can also use kitchen to prepare your own food.

The hostel is situated in the city center, but in a very calm neighborhood, where no street noise is heard. It’s only 10 minutes’ walk from the Republic Square, and 15 minutes’ walk from the Opera House. In 20 minutes you can get to Cascade complex. There is a bus station in 2 minutes walk from the hostel (the stuff will assist you with the bus number and direction). Many good cafes, restaurants and bars are situated in a walk distance.

We have a large garden with outdoor furniture and fruit trees. There you can spend warm evenings with your friends and even make a BBQ. There is a large lounge area also, where you can chill with friends, drink wine, play games, watch TV and just enjoy. Everything in our place is made to give you the best experience. We’ll be happy to assist you with any problem
